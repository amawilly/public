It Common using in our life

Agriculture:
In the sector of agriculture, information technology plays a significant role. Temperature, humidity, wind speed, and radiation are all monitored using cutting-edge computer technology, which also allows for a wireless link between mobile devices and the greenhouse.
Business:
The use of information technology allows the business community to react rapidly to changes in the environment. It also facilitates the timely arrival of new items on the market.
Education:
Teachers may use IT to produce appealing audio and video presentations that will keep students engaged in their studies and help them comprehend ideas better.
Healthcare:
IT is used to spot medical errors in the healthcare sector. Nurses can spend more time on patient care if they spend less time documenting patient care.
